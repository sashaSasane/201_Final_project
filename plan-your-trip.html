Key Planning Steps

Book Flights in Advance: Booking at least 2-3 months ahead can help find the best deals.

Step 1: Choose Your Destination

Do some research on destinations that excite you! Consider your interests, travel dates, and budget.

Step 2: Set Your Travel Dates

Decide how many days you’ll spend at your destination. Consider local holidays and festivals that might be happening during your stay!

Step 3: Plan Your Budget

Start by setting a daily budget for your trip. Consider the cost of flights, accommodation, food, and activities. Use travel budget calculators to help estimate costs.

Student Affiliate Opportunities

Affiliate marketing allows students to earn passive income by promoting travel companies they love. Build online income while sharing travel tools and resources you already use.

Here are some trusted travel companies that offer affiliate programs:

  • Booking.com Affiliate Partner Program – Earn money when people book hotels through your link.
  • Expedia Affiliate Program – Promote flights, hotels, and packages on your blog or website.
  • Skyscanner Affiliate Program – Great for flight comparison and travel deals.
  • CJ Affiliate (TripAdvisor, Priceline & more) – Access multiple travel brands through one network.
  • Amazon Associates – Recommend travel gear, backpacks, and accessories.
